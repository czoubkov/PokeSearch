About Us

What inspired us?

All four of us grew up with Pokémon, and I'm sure many of you reading have had the same experience. We played the games, we collected the cards, and we watched the show. Most APIs out there do seem like random data or pertain to more serious topics. We found out that PokéAPI exists, which gave us the idea to do something different and make a fun application dedicated to an important part of our childhoods.
Made using PokéAPI
